About Me

Hey! Thanks for sticking around.

I've got some time to tell you that I'm a Boston College graduate who studied English and economics but always wished she could spend more time on art.

At school, this meant filling up empty spaces in my schedule with digital art, animation, and graphic design classes, while in my free time, this means running an art Instagram (@aver.ni) and this website!

In terms of what kind of animation I like to watch, action-packed fight scenes and beautiful backgrounds make my heart race, but in terms of my personal style, I love capturing specific expressions and experimenting with cool color schemes.

I hope you've enjoyed what I have to offer as an artist, and I'd love to keep in touch! Contact me at jastk2018@gmail.com.
